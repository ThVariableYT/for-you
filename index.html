Mere dil ki sabse gehri baatein, sirf tumhare liye likhi gayi

Yahaan baith kar main perfect shabd dhoondne ki koshish kar raha tha — par jo feel kar raha hoon abhi, uske liye koi bhi shabd kaafi nahi. Isliye yeh sab kuch tumhare liye banaya, kyunki tum deserve karti ho ek simple "sorry" se bhi zyada.
"Maine galti ki, aur main poori zimmedari leta hoon. Koi bhi bahana kaafi nahi hai, aur main aisa dikhane ki koshish bhi nahi karunga. Mere liye sabse zyada maayne rakhti hai tum — aur yeh sochna ke maine tumhe dukh diya, yeh mujhe sabse zyada takleef deta hai."
Tumne mujhe itna pyaar diya — isse zyada toh maine kabhi socha bhi nahi tha ke deserve karunga. Aur jawab mein, maine tumhe disappoint kiya. Main bahut gehre, sachche aur dil se maafi maangta hoon, Payal. Isliye nahi ke pakda gaya. Isliye nahi ke farz tha. Balki isliye kyunki iss duniya mein tum hi sabse zyada maayne rakhti ho mere liye.
Main dekhta hoon ke kaise tum care karti ho, kaise tum smile karti ho, kaise tum har cheez ko garam aur pyaara bana deti ho. Main kabhi bhi woh wajah nahi banna chahta jisse woh smile gayab ho jaaye. Kabhi nahi, dobbara.

Tumhare liye mere waade 🌸

  • Main waada karta hoon ke har kadam se pehle sochunga, kyunki tum deserve karti ho koi aisa jo tumhe soch samajh kar chune, har roz.
  • Main waada karta hoon ke behtar baat cheet karunga — tumse baat karunga bajaye iske ke cheezein gather hokar galti ban jaayein.
  • Main waada karta hoon ke yeh dobara nahi hoga. Yeh sirf shabd nahi hain — yeh woh waada hai jo main tumhe aur humare rishte ko karta hoon.
  • Main waada karta hoon ke tumse zyada pyaar se pyaar karunga, zyada mehnat se tumhare saath rahunga, aur woh insaan banunga jo tum deserve karti ho.
  • Main waada karta hoon ke har roz, main koshish karunga ke is pyaar ke layak ban sakoon jo tum itne dil se mujhe deti ho.
Tum meri sabse pyaari ho, mera safe place ho, mera ghar ho. Yeh sochna ke apni bewakoofi se tumhe kho doon — yeh main bardasht nahi kar sakta. Main tumse pyaar karta hoon, Payal — zyada se zyada se keh nahi sakta.
